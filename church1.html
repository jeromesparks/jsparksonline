IT'S ALL ABOUT JESUS.

At Westover Hills Church, our mission is simple — we want everyone to know Jesus and accept Him as Lord and Savior. When a person accepts Christ and begins a new life, we call that Making New.

Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here! 2 Corinthians 5:17

When Christ-followers put God at the center of their lives and seek a strong relationship with Him, we call that Making Great. We are a church where everyone is invited to seek and experience Making New and Making Great moments in their lives.

 

WAYS TO GIVE:

1. GIVE ONLINE.

Give securely as a guest or registered user. Create an account to access your giving history, make a pledge, change your settings and more.

2. TEXT TO GIVE.

Text to give is as easy as it sounds. Simply text your donation amount to 210-610-2129. If you want to give beyond your tithe, use the keyword KINGDOM to help fund outreach efforts, building projects, and other initiatives.

3. GIVE IN SERVICE.

If you prefer to give by cash or check, you can always do that in service. Fill out a Give envelope, enclose your donation inside and drop it in the offering.
